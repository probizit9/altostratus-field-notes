How AI Content Generation Retainers Work

AI-powered content generation retainers are structured service packages where agencies use advanced language models (Claude, GPT-4) to produce high-quality first drafts across content types:

  • Blog posts and articles: 1,500-2,500 word pieces researched and drafted in 20-30 minutes
  • Social media content: Month-long calendars with captions generated in under an hour
  • Email campaigns: Multi-email sequences with A/B variants created in 30-45 minutes

  • Case studies and whitepapers: Research synthesis and structure completed in 1-2 hours

The agency's role shifts from creation to curation: providing strategic direction, brand voice refinement, fact-checking, and final polish. This is where human expertise creates the most value.
