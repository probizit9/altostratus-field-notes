What Makes AI-Generated Memes Funny

AI meme generators create humor by analyzing patterns from millions of existing memes. They understand:

  • Meme template conventions – knowing which formats work for specific types of jokes

The result is memes that feel authentic to internet culture while being generated in seconds rather than requiring human brainstorming and design work.
